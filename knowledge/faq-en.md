# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions — FYS Field Sales Rep FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions that come up during field visits.

---

## About the Brand

**Q: Is FYS really from Heineken?**
A: Yes. FYS is part of the Heineken Group's non-alcoholic beverage portfolio in Brazil.

**Q: Why have I never seen FYS commercials on TV?**
A: FYS has a different communication strategy — more digital and point-of-sale focused. It doesn't invest in traditional mass media. That's part of the positioning: no marketing hype, just the product speaking for itself.

**Q: Is FYS a new brand?**
A: It was launched in 2019 and relaunched with a new positioning in 2021. It's in a growth phase.

---

## About the Product

**Q: What's the difference between FYS and Coca-Cola Zero or Pepsi Black?**
A: FYS isn't zero — it has 50% less sugar (not zero). That's for people who want to cut back but don't want to give up actual flavor. Also, the brand positioning and tone are completely different: no exaggerated promises, no inflated marketing.

**Q: Does FYS have a zero sugar version?**
A: Yes. The FYS Zero line has zero sugar and zero calories.

**Q: What sizes are available?**
A: 350ml can and 600ml PET bottle, in both Original (50% less sugar) and Zero versions.

**Q: Does the product need refrigeration?**
A: Yes. Like all soft drinks, it should be served chilled. It can be stored at room temperature, but must go into the fridge before sale.

---

## About the Sale

**Q: What's the minimum quantity for a first order?**
A: Check the current commercial price sheet. Generally, the entry point is one case (12 units).

**Q: The owner wants a discount. What do I say?**
A: "The entry price is already a test price. What I can guarantee is that you won't lose on margin — and you'll test with very low risk."

**Q: What if the product doesn't sell?**
A: Make sure it's positioned correctly (chilled, visible, near the checkout). If it still doesn't sell in 2 weeks, talk to your supervisor about the return policy in your region.

**Q: Can I leave point-of-sale materials (display, sticker)?**
A: Depends on availability in your commercial kit. Check with your manager.

---

## About the Approach

**Q: How much time do I have per bakery visit?**
A: On average, 1 to 3 minutes. Get to the point fast. Use the copilot to have the right answer ready.

**Q: How do I start the conversation?**
A: Simple and direct. Example: *"Good morning! I have something new from the Heineken Group — not beer. Got 3 minutes?"*

**Q: Can the copilot help me build the full approach?**
A: Yes. Describe the establishment's profile and the copilot will suggest the most appropriate approach for that specific situation.
