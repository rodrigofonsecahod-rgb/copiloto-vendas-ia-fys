# 📖 FYS Brand Context

## What is FYS?

FYS is a sparkling beverage from the **Heineken Group**, launched in 2019 and relaunched in 2021. The brand was created to fill a space that traditional brands don't occupy: the consumer who wants an **honest option, with less sugar and without the usual marketing hype**.

---

## Product Data

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Group** | Heineken |
| **Launch** | 2019 (relaunched in 2021) |
| **Sugar content** | 50% less sugar (main line) / zero sugar (zero line) |
| **Format** | Individual consumption (350ml can, 600ml PET bottle) |
| **Segment** | Adult non-alcoholic sparkling beverage |
| **Positioning** | A real alternative for those who don't want Coke, but don't want water either |

---

## Brand Positioning

FYS **does not compete as a market leader**. It competes as **the different brand**:

- Doesn't promise what it can't deliver
- Doesn't pretend to be #1
- Talks to consumers like adults
- Uses sharp humor and self-irony as its identity

> "We're not the best-seller. But at least we don't tell you to choose health and then sell you sugar."

---

## Brand Tone of Voice

| What it IS | What it is NOT |
|---|---|
| Direct and to-the-point | Corporate and formal |
| Slightly sharp and witty | Aggressive or offensive |
| Honest about the product | Exaggerated and hyperbolic |
| Confident | Desperate or begging |
| Adult and intelligent | Childish or forced |

## The Bakery Channel Challenge

**The problem:** Bakeries are the **main soft drink retail point in Brazil**, but FYS has **less than 1% penetration** in that channel.

**Why?**
- Heineken's sales force prioritizes bars and nightclubs (higher beer volume)
- Sales reps have very little time per visit (1 to 3 minutes)
- There's a lack of fast, contextualized argumentation
- Coca-Cola and Guaraná Antarctica are already installed and requested by name

**Market data:**
- 72,000+ bakeries in Brazil
- Bakeries account for ~30% of soft drink volume in the country
- FYS penetrates <1% of that universe
- Each bakery fridge has space for roughly 6 soft drink SKUs

---

## The Opportunity

Every converted bakery represents a permanent point of exposure. A sales rep who convinces the owner to put 6 cans on the shelf is planting a seed of recurrence — if the product sells, the order repeats itself.

**AI can help the sales rep to:**
1. Arrive with the right arguments for each objection
2. Communicate FYS's value in under 1 minute
3. Not lose deals because they didn't know what to say

## Competitive Differentiators

| vs. Coca-Cola | vs. Guaraná Antarctica | vs. Water |
